About Me

Hello! I am a recent Mechanical Engineering graduate at Northeastern University and graduated in December 2025. I was apart of AeroNu, Northeastern's rocketry team, for 2 years and I have completed one Co-op at Barletta Engineering Heavy Division gaining 6 months of work experience. Since I was a kid I have always liked building and fixing things whether it was erector sets, legos, and working on car projects like brakes with my dad. This same passion is with me today but now with other engineers working on projects at Northeastern.

At Barletta I worked as a project engineer working on two main projects. Managing the improvements of Boston's red line train maintenance facility and a Bridge bundle project. On both these jobs I worked with the Massachusetts bay transit authority (MBTA), contractors, construction workers, and other engineers to ensure was completed on time and within budget as per the contract. I also reviewed project schedules, submitted RFI's and submittals, reviewed shop drawings, and compiled change orders.

Thus, I am looking forward to working in a full time position where the projects that I work on with others will help progress the human race and change the world for the better.
