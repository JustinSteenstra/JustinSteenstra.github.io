Blood/Water collision with Wall

(Computational Fluid Dynamics class Final)

Skills

CFD Fluent

Objective

Our objective was to perform a CFD Fluent analysis using the Volume of Fraction model to analyze the similarities and differences between a water and blood droplet collision

Process

My process was,

  • I first created the geometry of of a nozzle and a chamber and set the inside conditions to water and blood, respectively.
  • Then I meshed the geometry and solved to create a contour for the volume fraction of water to air at at increasing time steps of 6 micro seconds
  • And then I used the same geometry and mesh to solve for the volume fraction of blood to air at the same time steps.
  • However, this time I used the Cell-Viscosity user defined function to input the Carreau-Yasuda viscosity model for blood viscosity to create a new fluid.
